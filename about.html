"JAWAHARLAL COUNCIL FOR VOCATIONAL AND SKILL DEVELOPMENT MISSION (JCVSDM)" JAWAHARLAL SKILL CERTIFICATION COUNCIL (JSCC) is established on 2000.

JAWAHARLAL COUNCIL FOR VOCATIONAL AND SKILL DEVELOPMENT MISSION

ABOUT JAWAHARLAL COUNCIL

Based on the recommendations of Skill India Programme Jawaharlal Jawaharlal Council for Vocational and Skill Development Mission (Jawaharlal Skill Certification Council) is AN ISO 9001: 2015 under UASL accreditation Quality management system Regd.Under registration Act. 1882 Govt. INDIA. Government approved autonomous body regd. NGO under Govt. of INDIA, Recognized by Ministry of MSME, Government of India, New Delhi. JCVSDM Registered under NGO Partnership System, Niti aayog (Govt. of India).Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Govt. of India).Authorized by skills Indian institute of development technology, Certify that commitment central vigilance commission, Department of chemicals & petro chemicals, Under Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Govt. of India, Member of British council, Ministry of youth affairs and sports Govt. of India, Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology, Govt of India, Ministry of agriculture & farmers welfare Govt of india.

Based on the recommendations of Skill India Programme Jawaharlal Council, an Autonomous Body & a National Development Authority which promotes JCVSD Jawaharlal Council for Vocational and Skill Development Mission. Our Skill Certification Council not depend on any government body, any government bondation with our programmes. JCVSD Jawaharlal Council for Vocational and Skill Development Mission is a govt. authorized self-governing organization. JSCC-Jawaharlal Skill Certification Council is a division of JCVSD Jawaharlal Council for Vocational and Skill Development Mission registered under State Government. The Council has been established in 2000 with an aim to empower youth to take part in the economic and all round growth of India. We aim to offer comprehensive assessment facilities for a variety of courses which lends a supportive hand to the young aspiring individuals and help them secure a bright and prosperous living.

VISION

Our vision is to have a competent, innovative and nationally competitive national human resource with the ability to contribute to the socio-economic and educational advancement of the world. To provide a common platform for social, educational, economic, legal and environmental advancement of people in need.

MISSION

Our mission is to ensure open and equitable access to high- quality lifelong technical and vocational training of a nationally recognized standard, through the efficient and cost – effective use of resources, supporting economic diversification and growth. Provide education across world through channel.

Jawaharlal Council’s unique value proposition arises from the breadth of its offerings. Jawaharlal Council provides a host of Certificate, Diploma and Post Graduate, Trade Certifications, Diploma programs in the fields of Software and IT, TEACHER TRAINING, COMPUTER EDUCATION, VOCATIONAL EDUCATION, VOCATIONAL EDUCATION, HARDWARE NETWORKING, AGRICULTURE EDUCATION, FASHION DESIGN & TEXTILE, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, PARAMEDICAL SCIENCES, BIOMEDICAL TECHNOLOGY, MANAGEMENT EDUCATION, ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY, MASS COMMUNICATION AND JOURNALISM, ASTROLOGY AND VASTU, SOCIAL SCIENCE, FOOTWEAR TECHNOLOGY, SPORTS AND PHYSICAL EDUCATION, HANDWRITING TRAINING, SECURITY SERVICES TRAINING, FIRE AND SAFETY TRAINING, HOTEL AND HOSPITALITY MANAGEMENT, BEAUTY & WELLNESS, PERFORMING ART, INDUSTRIAL PROJECTS TRAINING, Arts and Commerce Training Courses provider across world.
